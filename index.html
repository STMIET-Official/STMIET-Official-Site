About Us

Powering Industries with Trust & Integrity

Seven-Trent Machineries Industrial Equipment Trading (STMIET) is engaged in the sale and distribution of industrial machinery and equipment, and the hauling and distribution of petroleum products. Founded on November 26, 2021, by Ermie Panosa Bagatua, a seasoned maritime professional with 34 years of overseas experience.

STMIET was established to address unsafe and illegal retailing of fuels and to provide communities with safe, legitimate petroleum supply. Today, we serve 200+ mini gas stations across Zamboanga del Norte, Zamboanga del Sur, Zamboanga City, Basilan, and Misamis Occidental.
